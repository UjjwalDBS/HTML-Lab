Sumo Salt

There are many religious traditions in sumo: wrestlers sip sacred water and throw purifying salt into the ring before a match; the referee dresses like a Shinto priest, a Shinto shrine hangs over the ring. ... The wrestling area is considered sacred and every time a wrestler enters the ring he must purify it with salt.
