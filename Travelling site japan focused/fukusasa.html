Fukusasa

Fukusasa are branches of leafy bamboo decorated with lucky trinkets and ornaments made by miko (shrine maidens). They are typically sold in January by shrines dedicated to Ebassen, the Shinto god of business prosperity. Business owners buy them in the hopes of inviting luck to their companies for the remainder of the year.
